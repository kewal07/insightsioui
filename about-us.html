About

In today’s Data Driven Business Ecosystem, Consumer Insights & Public Opinion form the basis for decision making and policy formulations. The ability to ‘Ask’ the people a question and measure what the majority has to say - is a tool that is firmly embedded in the fundamentals of Decision Making. The Power of Internet and Social Communities can empower organisations with a strong tool for Business Decisions. AskByPoll has been created with the intent to make the Opinion Gathering from Consumers Fast, Easy and Cost Effective
